About SGM

Samuel Ghuna Ministries is pioneered by Ps Samuel Ghuna of United Youth Network. Having started to minister among youths and working adults of the Indian Community in Malaysia since 30th July 2016.

He has been able to reach out and impact many young lives and families through motivational and crime awareness seminars, campaigns, and events. God's grace and Word has been a stronghold since day one hence has brought great people along the way to assist and bless the ministry.

He is also ministering through media channel through his own Gospel movies, Maatram 1: From Lock-Up to Look-Up a movie inspired based on his real life experience and the upcoming Maatram 2: The Choice is Your that is going through final production and will be released soon. 2018 events and camps are being organized following the movie theme,

Having grown to serve Malaysian Indian's through seminars and media. Samuel Ghuna Ministries has been an urge to be a home platform welcoming various people from different backgrounds to be part of the family following the movement to serve God and Glorify His Name.

Our Ministries

United Youth Network

United Network Ministries founded by Mr. Samuel Ghuna with mission for Malaysian Indian Youths. Committed to bring changes in the life of Indian Youths by enrolling them as a part time or fulltime (depends) in Government organization force.

Recognizing the area of needs, assist and help Indian Youths to excel further.  To cease as much as possible all erroneous activities and provide a solution to break out from the behaviours.
